Smartdesk

Smartdesk is a touch-based GUI application designed to simplify system monitoring and streamline custom interactions with your main PC. It allows you to view real-time resource usage—such as CPU, memory, and network activity—and launch applications directly from a secondary touch interface, like a tablet or mounted display. With Smartdesk, there's no need to use a keyboard or mouse on your main machine for routine tasks. It's perfect for keeping your workflow smooth and hands-free. More features and customization options are on the way!
